## What we collect

**When you place an order:** your name, email address, billing address,
shipping address, phone number, and the contents of your order.

**When you personalize a product:** the names, dates, and text you enter, and
any **photographs you upload**. These are personal data, and in the case of
photographs of people, they may reveal information about others too. Only
upload photographs you have the right to use.

**When you contact us:** your name, email address, and the content of your
message.

**When you browse:** your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and cookie
data. See our cookie banner for the full list and to manage your consent.

We do **not** see or store your full payment card details. Payments are
processed by Stripe, who receive them directly.

## Who we share it with

We share only what is necessary, with:

- **Printify** and its print providers — your name, shipping address, and your
  design files, so the item can be printed and shipped to you.
- **Customily** — your personalization data and uploaded images, to generate
  the print file.
- **Stripe** — payment processing.
- **[o teu hosting]** — website hosting.
- **[Google Analytics, se usares]** — anonymous usage statistics, only with
  your consent.

Some of these processors may transfer data outside the EU. Where they do, the
transfer is covered by Standard Contractual Clauses or an adequacy decision.

We never sell your personal data.

## Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectify** data that is inaccurate or incomplete
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten"), where we have no overriding
  legal obligation to keep it
- **Restrict** or **object to** our processing
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Withdraw consent** at any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these, email [contact@ourmoment.com]. We will respond
within one month.

If you believe we have mishandled your data, you may complain to the
Portuguese supervisory authority, the **Comissão Nacional de Proteção de
Dados (CNPD)** — www.cnpd.pt.
