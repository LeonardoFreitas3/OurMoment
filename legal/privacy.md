# Privacy Policy

Last updated: [data]

This policy explains what personal data OurMoment collects, why we collect it,
and what rights you have over it. We are the data controller.

We sell to customers in **Europe and the United States**. The core of this
policy applies to everyone; the sections marked **Europe** and **United States**
set out the extra rights you have depending on where you live.

**Address:** [endereço]
**Email:** [contact@ourmoment.com]
**VAT / Tax ID:** [número]

## What we collect

**When you place an order:** your name, email address, billing address,
shipping address, phone number, and the contents of your order.

**When you personalize a product:** the names, dates, and text you enter, and
any **photographs you upload**. These are personal data, and in the case of
photographs of people, they may reveal information about others too. Only
upload photographs you have the right to use.

**When you contact us:** your name, email address, and the content of your
message.

**When you browse:** your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and cookie
data. See our cookie banner for the full list and to manage your choices.

We do **not** see or store your full payment card details. Payments are
processed by Stripe, who receive them directly.

## Why we collect it

| Purpose | Why we are allowed to |
|---|---|
| Processing and delivering your order | Performance of a contract |
| Sending order and shipping confirmations | Performance of a contract |
| Sending your design to our print partner | Performance of a contract |
| Preventing fraud and abuse | Legitimate interest |
| Analytics and improving the site | Consent |
| Marketing emails | Consent, which you may withdraw at any time |
| Keeping accounting and tax records | Legal obligation |

> **Europe.** The right-hand column is our lawful basis under Article 6 GDPR.

## Who we share it with

We share only what is necessary, with:

- **Printful** and its fulfillment centers — your name, shipping address, and
  your design files, so the item can be printed and shipped to you.
- **Customily** — your personalization data and uploaded images, to generate
  the print file.
- **Stripe** — payment processing.
- **[o teu hosting]** — website hosting.
- **[Google Analytics, se usares]** — usage statistics, only with your consent.

**We do not sell your personal data**, and we do not share it for
cross-context behavioural advertising.

## Where your data goes

We fulfil orders from facilities on both sides of the Atlantic, so your data
may be processed in the **United States** and in the **European Union**,
depending on where your order is printed and shipped from.

> **Europe.** Where your data is transferred outside the EEA, the transfer is
> covered by an adequacy decision or by Standard Contractual Clauses.

- **Order and invoice records:** [X] years, as required by tax law.
- **Uploaded photographs and design files:** [90] days after your order ships,
  so we can reprint if something goes wrong. Then deleted.
- **Contact form messages:** [2] years.
- **Marketing consent:** until you withdraw it.
- **Analytics data:** [14] months.

## Your rights — everyone

Wherever you live, you can ask us to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** data that is inaccurate or incomplete
- **Delete** your data, where we have no overriding legal obligation to keep it
- **Withdraw consent** at any time, where processing is based on consent
- **Stop marketing emails**, via the unsubscribe link or by emailing us

Email [contact@ourmoment.com] to exercise any of these. We will not treat you
differently for asking.

## Your rights — Europe

Under the **GDPR** you additionally have the right to:

- **Restrict** or **object to** our processing
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Lodge a complaint** with your national data protection authority

We will respond to any request within **one month**.

If you believe we have mishandled your data, you may complain to the
supervisory authority in your country of residence. [Se a entidade for
portuguesa: Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados (CNPD) — www.cnpd.pt]

## Your rights — United States

If you are a resident of **California** (and, in substance, of other states
with comparable privacy laws), you additionally have the right to:

- **Know** what personal information we collect, use and disclose
- **Delete** the personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Opt out** of the sale or sharing of personal information — we do not sell
  or share it, so there is nothing to opt out of
- **Non-discrimination** — we will not deny you service, charge you a
  different price, or give you a lower quality of service for exercising
  any of these rights

We will respond to a verifiable request within **45 days**, and may extend
once where the request is complex.

## Cookies

We use cookies that are strictly necessary for the site and the cart to work,
and — only with your consent — cookies for analytics. You can manage your
choices at any time through the cookie banner.

> **Europe.** Non-essential cookies are set only after you opt in.
>
> **United States.** Our banner is configured for opt-out, as US law allows;
> you may decline non-essential cookies at any time.

## Children

Our store is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from
children under **16** (Europe) or under **13** (United States). If you believe
a child has given us personal data, email us and we will delete it.

## Changes

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date at the top will always
tell you when. Material changes will be announced on the site.
